Welcome To Lotus Aari Work

Welcome to Lotus Aari Work, a leading company in the field of Aari work with over 5 years of experience. Our company is dedicated to providing professional Aari embroidery training to students all over the world. We have had the privilege of training over 500 students in our time, and we are proud to offer our courses in two languages (Tamil & English) to make them accessible to a wider audience.

We Provide the Following
Classes:

  • Aari Embroidery Class
  • Aari Tracing Workshop
  • Saree Pre Platting Class
  • Fabric Painting Class
